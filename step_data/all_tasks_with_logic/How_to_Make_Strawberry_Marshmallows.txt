Step 1:
Previous scene: None
Action: Prepare ingredients: 1/2 cup fresh strawberry puree, 3 packages unflavored gelatin, 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar, 1 cup light corn syrup, 1/2 cup water, confectioners' sugar, and a pinch of salt. *

Step 2:
Previous scene: Ingredients prepared
Action: Line a 9x13 inch baking pan with parchment paper and dust it generously with confectioners' sugar. *

Step 3:
Previous scene: Lined and dusted baking pan
Action: In a mixing bowl, combine 1/2 cup of fresh strawberry puree and the unflavored gelatin. Let it sit for 10 minutes to bloom. *

Step 4: 
Previous scene: Strawberry puree and gelatin mixture
Action: In a saucepan, mix granulated sugar, light corn syrup, and water. Place it over medium heat and stir until the sugar dissolves completely. *

Step 5:
Previous scene: Sugar mixture in saucepan
Action: Increase the heat to high and cook without stirring until the mixture reaches 240°F on a candy thermometer. *

Step 6:
Previous scene: Sugar mixture at 240°F
Action: Remove the saucepan from heat and slowly pour the hot syrup into the gelatin mixture, while mixing on low speed. *

Step 7:
Previous scene: Gelatin mixture with hot syrup
Action: Increase the mixer speed to high and whip until the mixture becomes thick and cools to lukewarm, about 10-15 minutes. *

Step 8:
Previous scene: Thickened and cooled marshmallow mixture
Action: Pour the marshmallow mixture into the prepared baking pan and spread evenly. *

Step 9:
Previous scene: Marshmallow mixture in baking pan
Action: Dust the top with more confectioners' sugar and let it set at room temperature for at least 4 hours or overnight. *

Step 10:
Previous scene: Set marshmallows in baking pan
Action: Once set, lift the marshmallow slab out of the pan using the parchment paper. *

Step 11:
Previous scene: Marshmallow slab on parchment paper
Action: Cut the marshmallow slab into squares, and dust each side of the squares with more confectioners' sugar to prevent sticking. *

Step 12:
Previous scene: Marshmallow squares
Action: Store the strawberry marshmallows in an airtight container. Enjoy! *
