Step 1:
Image Description: A large pot on the stove is heating olive oil over medium heat. The oil is shimmering.
Action: In a large pot, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat *

Step 2:
Image Description: Chopped onions, minced garlic, and chopped celery are added to the hot oil in the pot. The vegetables are starting to sauté, releasing their aroma as they cook.
Action: Add 1 chopped onion, 2 minced garlic cloves, and 3 chopped celery stalks *

Step 3:
Image Description: The onions, garlic, and celery are sautéing in the pot, softening and turning lightly golden as they cook. The mixture is fragrant.
Action: Sauté until the vegetables are softened, about 5-7 minutes *

Step 4:
Image Description: Chopped carrots and diced bell peppers are added to the softened vegetables in the pot. The colorful vegetables mix together, adding texture and flavor to the sauté.
Action: Add 2 chopped carrots and 1 diced bell pepper *

Step 5:
Image Description: Dried thyme, dried basil, and dried oregano are being sprinkled over the vegetables. The herbs blend into the sautéed vegetables, enhancing the flavor with earthy, fragrant notes.
Action: Stir in 1 teaspoon of dried thyme, 1 teaspoon of dried basil, and 1 teaspoon of dried oregano *

Step 6:
Image Description: Six cups of vegetable broth are poured into the pot, covering the sautéed vegetables. The broth is starting to simmer, combining with the herbs and vegetables to create a flavorful soup base.
Action: Add 6 cups of vegetable broth *

Step 7:
Image Description: A can of diced tomatoes, including the liquid, is added to the pot. The tomatoes add a bright, tangy flavor to the broth, mixing with the vegetables and herbs.
Action: Add 1 can (15oz) of diced tomatoes, including the liquid *

Step 8:
Image Description: The pot of soup is brought to a boil, with bubbles rising vigorously. The heat is then reduced, and the soup begins to simmer gently, allowing the flavors to meld.
Action: Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes *

Step 9:
Image Description: Chopped zucchini and green beans are added to the simmering soup. The fresh vegetables float in the broth, adding vibrant color and texture to the soup.
Action: Add 1 cup of chopped zucchini and 1 cup of chopped green beans *

Step 10:
Image Description: The soup is simmering, with the zucchini and green beans softening as they cook. The vegetables are tender.
Action: Simmer for another 10-15 minutes, until all vegetables are tender *

Step 11:
Image Description: The soup is seasoned with salt and freshly ground black pepper, adjusting the flavor to taste. The seasoning blends into the broth, enhancing the overall taste of the soup.
Action: Season with salt and pepper to taste *

Step 12:
Image Description: The hot vegetable soup is served in a bowl, optionally garnished with fresh parsley for a pop of color and flavor. The soup looks hearty and nourishing.
Action: Serve hot, optionally garnished with fresh parsley *
