Step 1:
Image Description: The oven is preheating to 350°F (175°C), and a 9-inch round baking pan is greased with butter or oil, ready for the cake batter.
Action: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9-inch round baking pan. *

Step 2:
Image Description: A mixing bowl contains dry ingredients, including all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
Action: In a mixing bowl, combine 1 ⅓ cups of all-purpose flour, 1 ⅓ cups of granulated sugar, ½ cup of unsweetened cocoa powder, 1 ½ teaspoons of baking powder, and ½ teaspoon of salt. *

Step 3:
Image Description: Eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract are added to the dry ingredients in the mixing bowl, preparing the wet and dry ingredients to be mixed together.
Action: Add 2 large eggs, 1 cup of milk, ½ cup of vegetable oil, and 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract to the dry ingredients bowl. *

Step 4:
Image Description: An electric mixer is beating the mixture on medium speed, blending the ingredients into a smooth, rich chocolate cake batter.
Action: Using an electric mixer, beat the mixture on medium speed for about 2 minutes until well combined. *

Step 5:
Image Description: The chocolate cake batter is poured evenly into the prepared 9-inch round baking pan, filling it halfway.
Action: Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan. *

Step 6:
Image Description: The cake is baking in the oven, with the top rising and setting as it bakes. A toothpick is inserted to check for doneness, coming out clean.
Action: Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. *

Step 7:
Image Description: The baked chocolate cake cools in the pan for 10 minutes before being removed and transferred to a wire rack to cool completely.
Action: Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then remove it from the pan and let it cool completely on a wire rack. *

Step 8:
Image Description: Once the cake has cooled, a serrated knife is used to carefully slice the cake horizontally into three even layers.
Action: Once the cake is completely cool, use a serrated knife to slice the cake horizontally into 3 even layers. *

Step 9:
Image Description: A generous layer of cherry jam is spread across the bottom layer of the cake, adding a sweet and fruity filling.
Action: Spread a layer of cherry jam on the bottom layer of the cake. *

Step 10:
Image Description: Heavy cream and powdered sugar are whipped together until stiff peaks form, creating a fluffy whipped cream topping for the cake.
Action: Whip 2 cups of heavy cream with ¼ cup of powdered sugar until stiff peaks form. *

Step 11:
Image Description: A layer of whipped cream is spread over the cherry jam on the bottom cake layer, creating a creamy and fruity filling.
Action: Spread a layer of whipped cream over the cherry jam. *

Step 12:
Image Description: The second layer of the cake is placed on top of the whipped cream, and the process of spreading cherry jam and whipped cream is repeated.
Action: Place the second layer of the cake on top and repeat the process of spreading cherry jam and whipped cream. *

Step 13:
Image Description: The third and final layer of cake is added on top, and the entire cake is covered with the remaining whipped cream, creating a smooth, white frosting.
Action: Add the final cake layer on top, then cover the entire cake with the remaining whipped cream. *

Step 14:
Image Description: The cake is garnished with chocolate shavings and maraschino cherries, adding a decorative and traditional Black Forest cake touch.
Action: Garnish the cake with chocolate shavings and maraschino cherries. *

Step 15:
Image Description: The decorated Black Forest cake is placed in the refrigerator to chill for at least an hour, allowing the flavors to meld before serving.
Action: Refrigerate the cake for at least an hour before serving for the flavors to meld. *
