Step 2:
Image Description: In a small bowl, olive oil is mixed with the juice of 2 freshly squeezed lemons, 3 minced garlic cloves, and dried oregano. The ingredients are stirred together, creating a tangy, aromatic marinade.
Action: In a small bowl, mix together 1/4 cup olive oil, the juice of 2 lemons, 3 minced garlic cloves, and 1 teaspoon dried oregano *

Step 3:
Image Description: The marinade is poured over the chicken breasts, which are placed in a shallow dish. The chicken is fully coated in the lemony, garlic-infused mixture, absorbing the flavors.
Action: Pour the marinade over the chicken breasts in a shallow dish *

Step 4:
Image Description: The shallow dish is covered and placed in the refrigerator. The chicken breasts marinate, allowing the flavors of the garlic, lemon, and oregano to penetrate the meat.
Action: Cover the dish and refrigerate for 30 minutes to 2 hours *

Step 5:
Image Description: The grill is preheating to medium-high heat, with the grates beginning to heat up. 
Action: Preheat the grill to medium-high heat *

Step 6:
Image Description: The marinated chicken breasts are removed from the marinade and placed directly onto the hot grill. The chicken begins to sizzle as it hits the grates.
Action: Remove the chicken from the marinade and place it on the grill *

Step 7:
Image Description: The chicken breasts are grilling on each side. The chicken turns golden brown with defined grill marks as it cooks through, staying juicy on the inside.
Action: Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side or until completely cooked through *

Step 8:
Image Description: The grilled chicken breasts are removed from the grill and placed on a cutting board or plate. They are left to rest for a few minutes, allowing the juices to settle before serving.
Action: Remove the grilled chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes before serving *
