Step 3:
Image Description: A mixing bowl holds flour, sugar, butter, milk, eggs, baking powder, and vanilla extract, ready to be mixed.
Action: In a mixing bowl, combine 2 cups of flour, 1 1/2 cups of sugar, 1/2 cup of unsalted butter, 1 cup of milk, 3 eggs, 1 tablespoon of baking powder, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract. *

Step 4:
Image Description: The ingredients are mixed together, creating a smooth and creamy cake batter with no lumps.
Action: Mix until the batter is smooth and well combined. *

Step 5:
Image Description: The smooth batter is poured into the prepared heart-shaped cake pan, filling it evenly.
Action: Pour the batter into the prepared heart-shaped cake pan. *

Step 6:
Image Description: The heart-shaped cake pan filled with batter is placed into the preheated oven.
Action: Place the pan in the preheated oven. *

Step 7:
Image Description: The cake is baking in the oven, rising evenly as the surface turns golden brown. A toothpick is inserted into the center to check for doneness.
Action: Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. *

Step 8:
Image Description: The cake is removed from the oven and left to cool in the heart-shaped pan for 10 minutes, allowing the cake to set before removing.
Action: Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes. *

Step 9:
Image Description: The heart-shaped cake is carefully removed from the pan and placed on a wire rack to cool completely, keeping its shape intact.
Action: Carefully remove the cake from the pan and let it cool completely on a wire rack. *

Step 10:
