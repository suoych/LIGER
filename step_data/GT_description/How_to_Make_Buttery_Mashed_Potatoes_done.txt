Action: Place the potato chunks in a large pot and cover them with cold water. *

Action: Bring the pot to a boil over high heat, then reduce to a simmer and cook until potatoes are tender (about 15-20 minutes). *

Step 4:

Image Description: The cooked potatoes are drained in a colander, with steam rising as the water is removed, leaving the tender potatoes behind.
Action: Drain the potatoes in a colander. *

Step 5:

Image Description: The drained potatoes are returned to the pot, and melted butter is added, beginning the mashing process.
Action: Return the potatoes to the pot and add 1/2 cup of melted butter. *

Step 6:

Image Description: The potatoes are mashed using a potato masher, creating a smooth, creamy consistency as the butter blends in.
Action: Mash the potatoes until smooth. *

Step 7:

Image Description: Warm milk is poured into the mashed potatoes, and the mixture is stirred until fully combined, making the potatoes even creamier.
Action: Add 1/4 cup of warm milk and stir until well combined. *

Step 8:

Image Description: The mashed potatoes are seasoned with salt and pepper to taste, then served warm, with a smooth and fluffy texture.
Action: Season with salt and pepper to taste, then serve warm. *
