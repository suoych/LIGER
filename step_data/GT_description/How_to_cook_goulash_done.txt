Image Description: A large pot is heating over medium heat with 2 tablespoons of oil shimmering.
Action: In a large pot, heat 2 tablespoons of oil over medium heat. *

Step 2:

Image Description: Diced onions are added to the pot, turning translucent as they sauté in the hot oil.
Action: Add 1 diced onion and sauté until translucent. *

Step 3:

Image Description: Beef cubes are added to the pot, browning on all sides while the onions continue to cook.
Action: Add 1 pound of beef cubes and brown them on all sides. *

Step 4:

Image Description: Paprika, caraway seeds, and minced garlic are stirred into the beef and onion mixture, creating a fragrant and flavorful base.
Action: Stir in 2 tablespoons of paprika, 1 teaspoon of caraway seeds, and 2 minced garlic cloves. *

Step 5:

Image Description: Beef broth and chopped bell peppers are poured into the pot, mixing with the seasoned beef and onions.
Action: Pour in 4 cups of beef broth and add 2 chopped bell peppers. *

Step 6:

Image Description: Diced tomatoes are added to the pot, and the mixture simmers, allowing the flavors to meld together.
Action: Add 2 diced tomatoes and let the mixture simmer for about an hour. *

Step 7:

Image Description: Diced potatoes are stirred into the pot, cooking until tender, adding heartiness to the goulash.
Action: Stir in 1 cup of diced potatoes and cook until potatoes are tender. *

Step 8:

Image Description: The finished goulash is seasoned with salt and pepper to taste and is rich and flavorful.
Action: Season with salt and pepper to taste, then serve. *
