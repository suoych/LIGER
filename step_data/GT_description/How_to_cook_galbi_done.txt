Image Description: Soy sauce is poured into a mixing bowl, forming the base for the marinade.
Action: In a mixing bowl, combine 1/2 cup soy sauce. *

Step 2:

Image Description: Sesame oil is added to the bowl, creating a fragrant, savory base for the marinade.
Action: Add 1/4 cup sesame oil. *

Step 3:

Image Description: Sugar is stirred into the soy sauce and sesame oil, adding sweetness to balance the flavors.
Action: Stir in 1/4 cup sugar. *

Step 4:

Image Description: Chopped garlic is added to the bowl, enhancing the marinade with a rich, aromatic flavor.
Action: Add 1/4 cup chopped garlic. *

Step 5:

Image Description: Freshly chopped green onions are mixed into the marinade, adding brightness and depth to the sauce.
Action: Add 1/4 cup chopped green onions. *

Step 6:

Image Description: Rice wine is stirred into the bowl, providing a subtle tang to the mixture.
Action: Stir in 1/4 cup rice wine. *

Step 7:

Image Description: Pear puree is added to the marinade, giving the mixture a slightly sweet, tenderizing quality.
Action: Add 1/4 cup pear puree. *

Step 8:

Image Description: Beef short ribs are submerged in the marinade, allowing them to soak up.
Action: Marinate 2 lbs of beef short ribs in the mixture for at least 4 hours. *

Step 9:

Image Description: The grill is preheated to medium-high heat, getting ready to cook the marinated beef short ribs.
Action: Preheat grill to medium-high heat. *

Step 10:

Image Description: The marinated beef short ribs are placed on the hot grill, cooking for 5-7 minutes on each side until tender and caramelized.
Action: Grill the marinated beef short ribs for about 5-7 minutes on each side or until cooked through. *

Step 11:

Image Description: The grilled galbi is served hot, accompanied by traditional side dishes like rice and kimchi.
Action: Serve the galbi hot with side dishes like rice and kimchi. *
