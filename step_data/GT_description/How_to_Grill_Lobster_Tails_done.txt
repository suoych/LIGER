Step 1:
Image Description: The grill is preheating to medium-high heat, with the temperature gauge rising to around 400°F (200°C). The grates are clean and heating up for cooking.
Action: Preheat the grill to medium-high heat (about 400°F or 200°C). *

Step 2:
Image Description: Whole lobster tails are being cut in half lengthwise with sharp kitchen shears, exposing the tender flesh inside. The lobster shells are vibrant red, and the meat is fresh and firm.
Action: Prepare the lobster tails by cutting them in half lengthwise using a sharp knife or kitchen shears. *

Step 3:
Image Description: The exposed lobster meat is brushed generously with melted butter, olive oil, or a marinade, giving it a glossy sheen. This will help the lobster stay moist during grilling.
Action: Brush the exposed flesh with melted butter, olive oil, or a marinade of your choice. *

Step 4:
Image Description: The brushed lobster tails are seasoned with salt, pepper, and additional spices or herbs like paprika or parsley, adding extra flavor to the tender meat.
Action: Season the lobster tails with salt, pepper, and any other desired spices or herbs. *

Step 5:
Image Description: The lobster tails are placed flesh side down on the hot grill grates. The flesh sizzles slightly as it makes contact with the heat, starting the cooking process.
Action: Place the lobster tails flesh side down on the grill grates. *

Step 6:
Image Description: The lobster tails are grilling, with the exposed flesh getting char marks. The lobster is flipped over, revealing the cooked flesh with beautiful grill lines.
Action: Grill the lobster tails for about 5-6 minutes, then flip them over. *

Step 7:
Image Description: The lobster tails are now grilling with the shell side down. The lobster meat is opaque and firm, indicating that it’s fully cooked.
Action: Grill for an additional 3-5 minutes, or until the lobster meat is opaque and firm. *

Step 8:
Image Description: The fully cooked lobster tails are removed and served immediately. The dish is garnished with fresh lemon wedges and a side of melted butter for dipping.
Action: Remove the lobster tails from the grill and serve immediately with lemon wedges and additional melted butter if desired. *
