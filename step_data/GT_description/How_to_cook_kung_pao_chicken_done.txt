Step 1:

Image Description: Boneless, skinless chicken breast is being cut into small, bite-sized pieces.
Action: Cut 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breast into bite-sized pieces. *

Step 2:

Image Description: Chicken pieces are marinating in a bowl with a mixture of soy sauce, rice vinegar, hoisin sauce, and cornstarch.
Action: In a bowl, combine 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of rice vinegar, 1 tablespoon of hoisin sauce, and 1 teaspoon of cornstarch, then marinate the chicken pieces in the mixture for at least 20 minutes. *

Step 3:

Image Description: Red bell pepper, green bell pepper, and a small onion are roughly chopped, ready for stir-frying.
Action: Roughly chop 1 red bell pepper, 1 green bell pepper, and 1 small onion. *

Step 4:

Image Description: Garlic cloves and fresh ginger are minced on a cutting board.
Action: Mince 3 cloves of garlic and 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger. *

Step 5:

Image Description: Oil is heating in a pan over medium-high heat.
Action: In a pan, heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. *

Step 6:

Image Description: The marinated chicken pieces are being cooked in the pan, turning lightly browned and cooked through.
Action: Add the marinated chicken to the pan and cook until lightly browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. *

Step 7:

Image Description: The cooked chicken pieces are removed from the pan and set aside, leaving the pan.
Action: Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside. *

Step 8:

Image Description: Chopped bell peppers and onion are stir-fried in the pan until tender-crisp, absorbing the flavors left behind from the chicken.
Action: In the same pan, add the chopped vegetables and stir-fry until they are tender-crisp, about 4-5 minutes. *

Step 9:

Image Description: Minced garlic and ginger are added to the stir-fried vegetables, cooking for an additional 1-2 minutes.
Action: Add the minced garlic and ginger to the pan and cook for another 1-2 minutes. *

Step 10:

Image Description: The cooked chicken is returned to the pan, mixing with the vegetables and garlic-ginger mixture.
Action: Return the chicken to the pan with the vegetables. *

Step 11:

Image Description: Roasted peanuts and optional dried red chilies are added to the chicken and vegetable stir-fry, enhancing the dishâ€™s texture and flavor.
Action: Add 1/2 cup of roasted peanuts and 2-3 dried red chilies (optional) to the pan. *

Step 12:

Image Description: Everything is stirred together in the pan, cooking to blend the flavors.
Action: Stir everything together and cook for another 2-3 minutes to combine flavors. *

Step 13:

Image Description: The finished Kung Pao Chicken is served over steamed rice, garnished with sliced green onions and extra peanuts.
Action: Serve the Kung Pao Chicken over steamed rice and garnish with sliced green onions and extra peanuts if desired. *
