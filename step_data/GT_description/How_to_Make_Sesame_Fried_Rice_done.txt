Step 1:
Image Description: A bowl of fluffy, cooked jasmine rice, fully cooled. The individual grains are separate and light.
Action: Cook 2 cups of jasmine rice according to package instructions and let it cool completely *

Step 2:
Image Description: A large skillet or wok on medium-high heat with a thin layer of shimmering sesame oil evenly coating the surface. The oil releases a nutty aroma as it heats.
Action: Heat 2 tablespoons of sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat *

Step 3:
Image Description: Finely chopped onions and minced garlic are added to the hot sesame oil. The onions are small and uniform, and the garlic is finely minced, sizzling slightly as they hit the oil.
Action: Add 1 finely chopped onion and 2 minced garlic cloves *

Step 4:
Image Description: The onions have turned translucent and are lightly golden, while the garlic is fragrant and slightly browned. The mixture is soft and caramelized.
Action: Sauté until the onion becomes translucent *

Step 5:
Image Description: The cooked and cooled jasmine rice is added to the skillet with the sautéed onions and garlic. The rice spreads across the pan, with the grains evenly dispersed among the aromatics.
Action: Add the cooked rice to the skillet *

Step 6:
Image Description: The rice is being stir-fried in the skillet, with some grains starting to crisp and turn lightly golden as they heat through. The mixture becomes slightly toasted, adding texture to the dish.
Action: Stir-fry the rice for a few minutes to heat through and slightly crisp up *

Step 7:
Image Description: The rice is pushed to one side of the skillet, and in the empty space, two beaten eggs are added. The eggs begin to cook and set at the edges, preparing to be scrambled.
Action: Push the rice to one side of the skillet and add 2 beaten eggs to the empty space *

Step 8:
Image Description: The eggs are fully scrambled, soft and fluffy. They are now being mixed into the rice, combining with the grains and onions for an even distribution.
Action: Scramble the eggs until fully cooked, then mix them into the rice *

Step 9:
Image Description: Bright green peas and small orange cubes of carrots are added to the skillet. The frozen vegetables are mixed into the rice and eggs, gradually heating through as they stir-fry.
Action: Add 1 cup of frozen peas and carrots, and stir-fry until they are heated through *

Step 10:
Image Description: Soy sauce is being drizzled over the stir-fried rice, giving it a rich brown color. Sesame seeds are sprinkled on top, adding texture and a slight nutty flavor to the dish.
Action: Drizzle 3 tablespoons of soy sauce and 1 tablespoon of sesame seeds over the rice mixture and stir well *

Step 11:
Image Description: The rice mixture is being stir-fried, evenly coated with soy sauce and sesame seeds. The rice has a glossy appearance, and the dish is almost ready.
Action: Continue to stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes, then remove from heat *

Step 12:
Image Description: The finished sesame fried rice is served hot, with a garnish of freshly chopped green onions on top. The dish is colorful, with the bright vegetables, golden eggs, and sesame seeds complementing the seasoned rice.
Action: Serve the sesame fried rice hot, garnished with chopped green onions if desired *
