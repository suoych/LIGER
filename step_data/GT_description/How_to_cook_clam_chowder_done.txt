Step 1:

Image Description: Butter is melting in a large pot over medium heat, forming a rich base for sautéing the onions and garlic.
Action: In a large pot, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. *

Step 2:

Image Description: Chopped onions and minced garlic are added to the pot, sautéing in the melted butter until the onions turn translucent and fragrant.
Action: Add 1 chopped onion, 2 minced garlic cloves, and sauté until onions are translucent. *

Step 3:

Image Description: Flour is sprinkled into the pot, creating a roux as it mixes with the butter and vegetables.
Action: Add 3 tablespoons of flour to the pot, stirring continuously to create a roux. *

Step 4:

Image Description: Clam juice is gradually poured into the roux mixture, thickening into a smooth base.
Action: Gradually pour in 4 cups of clam juice, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens. *

Step 5:

Image Description: Diced potatoes and celery are added to the thickened mixture, cooking in the broth until they become tender and absorb the flavors.
Action: Add 2 cups of diced potatoes, 2 cups of diced celery, and cook until the vegetables are tender. *

Step 6:

Image Description: Two cans of minced clams, along with their juice, are stirred into the soup base, adding a fresh, seafood flavor to the mixture.
Action: Stir in 2 cans of minced clams with their juice. *

Step 7:

Image Description: Creamy half-and-half is poured into the pot, giving the chowder a rich, creamy consistency.
Action: Add 2 cups of half-and-half cream to the pot. *

Step 8:

Image Description: Salt, pepper, and thyme are added to the pot, seasoning the clam chowder to taste, enhancing its flavors.
Action: Season the chowder with salt, pepper, and thyme to taste. *

Step 9:

Image Description: The chowder simmers on low heat for 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
Action: Allow the chowder to simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. *

Step 10:

Image Description: The hot clam chowder is served, optionally garnished with chopped parsley or crispy bacon bits, adding extra flavor and texture.
Action: Serve hot, optionally garnishing with chopped parsley or crispy bacon bits. *
