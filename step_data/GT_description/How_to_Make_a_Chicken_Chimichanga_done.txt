Step 1:
Image Description: A large skillet is on the stovetop with a tablespoon of oil heating over medium heat. The oil glistens and lightly coats the surface of the pan.
Action: Take a large skillet and heat 1 tablespoon of oil over medium heat. *

Step 2:
Image Description: Chopped onions are added to the skillet. The onions are softening as they cook, becoming slightly translucent and releasing a savory aroma.
Action: Add 1 chopped onion and cook until softened. *

Step 3:
Image Description: Two minced garlic cloves are added to the softened onions. The garlic is cooking for a minute, with its fragrant aroma filling the air and blending with the onions.
Action: Add 2 minced garlic cloves and cook for another minute. *

Step 4:
Image Description: Shredded, cooked chicken is added to the skillet, mixing with the softened onions and garlic. The chicken is golden and moist, evenly dispersed among the vegetables.
Action: Add 1 pound of shredded cooked chicken. *

Step 5:
Image Description: The chicken mixture is now combined with salsa, cumin, and chili powder. The salsa adds a rich, red color, while the spices blend into the mix, coating the chicken and creating a flavorful base.
Action: Stir in 1 cup of salsa, 1 teaspoon of cumin, and 1 teaspoon of chili powder. *

Step 6:
Image Description: The chicken mixture continues to cook in the skillet until it’s heated through. The salsa and spices meld into the chicken, and the entire mixture is hot.
Action: Let the mixture cook until heated through, then remove from heat. *

Step 7:
Image Description: A large flour tortilla is laid flat on a clean surface. Its surface is smooth, soft, and ready to be filled with the warm chicken mixture.
Action: Place a large flour tortilla on a flat surface. *

Step 8:
Image Description: About ¼ cup of the chicken mixture is spooned into the center of the tortilla. The mixture is well-balanced, with the shredded chicken, salsa, and spices nestled in the middle.
Action: Spoon about ¼ cup of chicken mixture into the center of the tortilla. *

Step 9:
Image Description: The tortilla is folded in on both sides, and then rolled up tightly, enclosing the filling completely. The edges are sealed, creating a neatly wrapped chimichanga.
Action: Fold in the sides of the tortilla, then roll up the ends to enclose the filling. *

Step 10:
Image Description: Another tablespoon of oil is heating in the skillet, shimmering slightly as it spreads evenly across the surface.
Action: Heat another tablespoon of oil in the skillet over medium heat. *

Step 11:
Image Description: The rolled chimichangas are placed seam-side down in the hot skillet. The oil sizzles as the chimichangas begin to fry, turning golden and crisp.
Action: Place the rolled chimichangas seam side down in the skillet. *

Step 12:
Image Description: The chimichangas are frying in the skillet, becoming golden-brown and crispy. One side is cooked, and they are flipped to fry the other side for even crispness.
Action: Fry until golden and crisp, about 2-3 minutes per side. *

Step 13:
Image Description: The golden, crispy chimichangas are removed from the skillet and plated. They are served with dollops of guacamole, sour cream, and extra salsa on the side.
Action: Serve with guacamole, sour cream, and extra salsa on the side. *
