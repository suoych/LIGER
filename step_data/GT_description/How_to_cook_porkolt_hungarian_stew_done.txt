Step 1:

Image Description: All ingredients are prepared and laid out on the countertop, including cubed pork, chopped onions, minced garlic, chopped bell pepper, diced tomatoes, paprika, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
Action: Prepare all your ingredients, including 1 kg of pork (cut into cubes), 2 onions (chopped), 2 cloves garlic (minced), 1 bell pepper (chopped), 2 tomatoes (diced), 2 tablespoons paprika, 1 tablespoon olive oil, salt, and pepper. *

Step 2:

Image Description: A large pot with olive oil heating over medium heat, the oil shimmering.
Action: Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. *

Step 3:

Image Description: Chopped onions are added to the pot, sautéing as they begin to turn translucent and soft.
Action: Add the chopped onions and sauté until they are translucent. *

Step 4:

Image Description: Minced garlic is added to the pot with the onions, sautéing for another minute as the aroma intensifies.
Action: Stir in the minced garlic and sauté for another minute. *

Step 5:

Image Description: Cubed pork is added to the pot with the sautéed onions and garlic. The pork is browning evenly on all sides, adding a rich, golden color to the dish.
Action: Add the pork cubes to the pot and cook until the meat is browned on all sides. *

Step 6:

Image Description: Paprika is mixed into the pot, coating the browned pork cubes, onions, and garlic in a vibrant red color as it blends into the dish.
Action: Mix in the paprika and stir well to coat the pork. *

Step 7:

Image Description: Chopped bell peppers and diced tomatoes are added to the pot, mixing with the pork and spices, creating a colorful mixture.
Action: Add the chopped bell pepper and diced tomatoes to the pot. *

Step 8:

Image Description: The dish is seasoned with salt and pepper, with the seasoning being mixed thoroughly into the pot to enhance the flavor of the stew.
Action: Season with salt and pepper to taste. *

Step 9:

Image Description: Water is added to the pot, covering the ingredients. The mixture is brought to a boil before the heat is reduced and the pot is covered to simmer.
Action: Add enough water to just cover the ingredients, bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and cover. Let it simmer for about 1 to 1.5 hours. *

Step 10:

Image Description: The stew is simmering uncovered, the meat tender and the liquid reducing slightly as it thickens to a rich consistency.
Action: Check for tenderness. If the meat is tender, let it simmer uncovered to thicken the stew. *

Step 11:

Image Description: The hot, rich stew is ready to be served with a side of noodles or dumplings, the tender pork and thickened sauce creating a hearty, flavorful meal.
Action: Serve hot with your choice of side, such as noodles or dumplings. *
