Image Description: A grill’s temperature gauge is set to medium-high heat, with the grates clean and glowing.
Action: Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. *

Step 2:

Image Description: Raw chicken breasts or thighs are laid out on a plate, generously seasoned with a sprinkling of salt and freshly ground black pepper. The seasoning is evenly distributed across the surface of the meat.
Action: Season the chicken with salt and pepper. *

Step 3:

Image Description: The seasoned chicken is placed directly onto the hot grill grates. A slight sizzle is heard as the chicken begins to cook, with grill marks already forming.
Action: Place the chicken on the grill. *

Step 4:

Image Description: The chicken is cooking on one side, with golden-brown grill marks visible as the meat cooks. The edges begin to turn opaque.
Action: Cook the chicken for about 5-7 minutes on one side. *

Step 5:

Image Description: The chicken is flipped over with grill tongs, revealing perfectly seared grill marks on the cooked side. The other side begins to cook through.
Action: Flip the chicken over to cook the other side. *

Step 6:

Image Description: A basting brush is used to generously coat the top side of the chicken with a layer of BBQ sauce. The sauce clings to the chicken, beginning to caramelize from the grill’s heat.
Action: Brush the chicken with BBQ sauce. *

Step 7:

Image Description: The chicken is flipped again, exposing the side coated with BBQ sauce, which is caramelizing and browning. The other side is then brushed with BBQ sauce to ensure full coverage.
Action: Flip the chicken again and brush the other side with BBQ sauce. *

Step 8:

Image Description: The BBQ-coated chicken continues to cook, with the sauce caramelizing and the meat browning perfectly. A meat thermometer is inserted, showing the internal temperature.
Action: Continue cooking until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F. *

Step 9:

Image Description: The chicken is lifted off the grill using tongs, the BBQ sauce now perfectly caramelized. The chicken is placed on a serving platter.
Action: Remove the chicken from the grill. *

Step 10:

Image Description: The grilled BBQ chicken rests on a plate for a few minutes, allowing the juices to redistribute throughout the meat. The sauce glistens on the surface.
Action: Let the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving. *
