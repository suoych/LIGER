Image Description: A bowl filled with finely chopped cabbage, scallions, and ginger is mixed with ground pork, creating a colorful, textured filling for the potstickers.
Action: Prepare the filling ingredients by chopping cabbage, scallions, and ginger finely. Mix them with ground pork in a bowl. *

Step 2:

Image Description: The filling is seasoned with soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper, and thoroughly mixed, giving the pork mixture a savory, aromatic scent.
Action: Season the filling with soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper, and mix thoroughly. *

Step 3:

Image Description: A round potsticker wrapper is laid flat, and a spoonful of the seasoned pork filling is placed neatly in the center.
Action: Take a potsticker wrapper and place a spoonful of filling in the center. *

Step 4:

Image Description: The edges of the wrapper are moistened with water, then folded in half, and sealed with pleats along the edges, creating a perfectly formed potsticker.
Action: Moisten the edges of the wrapper with water, fold it in half, and press to seal. Create pleats along the edges. *

Step 5:

Image Description: The skillet heats up with oil over medium-high heat, and the freshly formed potstickers are placed inside.
Action: Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat and place the potstickers in the pan. *

Step 6:

Image Description: The potstickers sizzle in the skillet, their bottoms turning a beautiful golden brown as they fry.
Action: Fry the potstickers until the bottoms are golden brown. *

Step 7:

Image Description: Water is added to the skillet, causing steam to rise as the potstickers are covered, allowing them to steam and cook through.
Action: Add water to the skillet and cover it to steam the potstickers. *

Step 8:

Image Description: The lid is removed, and the potstickers continue to cook as the water evaporates, leaving the potstickers fully cooked and slightly crisp.
Action: Remove the lid and continue cooking until all the water has evaporated. *

Step 9:

Image Description: The potstickers are transferred to a serving plate, golden brown and steaming hot, ready to be served with a savory dipping sauce.
Action: Transfer the potstickers to a serving plate and serve with dipping sauce. *
