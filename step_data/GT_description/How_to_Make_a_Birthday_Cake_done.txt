Step 1:
Image Description: A modern metal oven is slightly open with the display showing 350°F. The interior oven light softly illuminates the empty metal racks inside, indicating the oven is warming up.
Action: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). *

Step 2:
Image Description: Two 9-inch round cake pans are greased with butter or oil, and then lightly dusted with flour.
Action: Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans. *

Step 3:
Image Description: In a large mixing bowl, sugar, flour, and cocoa powder are combined. The dry ingredients create a rich, dark brown mixture.
Action: In a mixing bowl, combine 2 cups of sugar, 1-3/4 cups of flour, 3/4 cup of cocoa powder. *

Step 4:
Image Description: Baking powder, baking soda, and salt are added to the dry ingredients, ready to help the cake rise evenly during baking.
Action: Add 1-1/2 teaspoons of baking powder, 1-1/2 teaspoons of baking soda, and 1 teaspoon of salt. *

Step 5:
Image Description: Eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract are added to the bowl with the dry ingredients, making the batter thick and smooth as it’s mixed together.
Action: Add 2 eggs, 1 cup of milk, 1/2 cup of vegetable oil, and 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract. *

Step 6:
Image Description: The mixture is beaten on medium speed, turning into a thick, rich cake batter as the ingredients blend together.
Action: Mix on medium speed for 2 minutes. *

Step 7:
Image Description: Boiling water is carefully stirred into the batter, making the mixture thinner and smoother.
Action: Stir in 1 cup of boiling water (batter will be thin). *

Step 8:
Image Description: The thin batter is poured evenly into the two prepared cake pans, filling them about halfway.
Action: Pour the batter evenly into the prepared cake pans. *

Step 9:
Image Description: The cake pans are placed in the oven, and the cakes are baked. The cakes rise and become fluffy, and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean when done.
Action: Bake for 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. *

Step 10:
Image Description: The baked cakes cool in their pans, then are gently removed and transferred to a wire rack to cool completely.
Action: Allow cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove them from the pans and place on a wire rack to cool completely. *

Step 11:
Image Description: In a mixing bowl, softened butter, powdered sugar, heavy cream, and vanilla extract are beaten together to create a smooth, fluffy frosting.
Action: Prepare frosting by beating together 1 cup of softened butter, 3-4 cups of powdered sugar, 1/4 cup of heavy cream, and 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract. *

Step 12:
Image Description: Once the cakes are completely cool, one layer is placed on a serving plate, and a generous layer of frosting is spread over the top.
Action: Once the cakes are completely cool, place one cake layer on a serving plate and spread a layer of frosting on top. *

Step 13:
Image Description: The second cake layer is placed on top of the frosted bottom layer, and the remaining frosting is spread over the top and sides, covering the entire cake.
Action: Place the second layer on top and spread the remaining frosting over the top and sides of the cake. *

Step 14:
Image Description: The frosted two-layer cake is decorated with sprinkles, candles, or other decorations, giving it a festive appearance.
Action: Decorate the cake as desired with sprinkles, candles, or other decorations. *

Step 15:
Image Description: The decorated cake is sliced into pieces, revealing moist layers of chocolate cake.
Action: Slice and enjoy! *
