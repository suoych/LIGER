Action: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). *

Step 2:

Image Description: Several raw chicken breasts are placed on a cutting board, their pale pink flesh smooth and plump, ready for seasoning.
Action: Place the chicken breasts on a cutting board. *

Step 3:

Image Description: The chicken breasts are sprinkled with coarse salt and freshly cracked black pepper, creating a light dusting of seasoning across the surface of the meat.
Action: Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. *

Step 4:

Image Description: A skillet is heated over medium-high heat, and a tablespoon of olive oil is added, spreading evenly and shimmering as it warms up.
Action: Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add 1 tablespoon of olive oil. *

Step 5:

Image Description: The seasoned chicken breasts are placed into the hot skillet, immediately sizzling as they begin to sear. The edges start to turn golden brown after a few minutes of cooking.
Action: Sear the chicken breasts in the skillet for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown. *

Step 6:

Image Description: The seared chicken breasts are transferred from the skillet to a baking dish, their surfaces golden and slightly crispy, ready to bake.
Action: Transfer the seared chicken breasts to a baking dish. *

Step 7:

Image Description: The baking dish, filled with the chicken breasts, is placed on the middle rack of the preheated oven. The oven door closes, and the chicken begins to cook through.
Action: Place the baking dish in the preheated oven. *

Step 8:

Image Description: The chicken breasts are baking in the oven, their golden tops becoming firmer as they cook. The internal temperature gradually rises, and a thermometer inserted into one reaches 165°F (74°C), signaling they are done.
Action: Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165°F (74°C). *

Step 9:

Image Description: The cooked chicken breasts are placed on the countertop to rest. Steam rises gently from the chicken as it cools slightly, allowing the juices to redistribute.
Action: Remove the chicken breasts from the oven and let them rest for 5 minutes. *

Step 10:

Image Description: A sharp knife slices through the rested chicken breasts, revealing tender, juicy meat inside. The slices are ready to be served, perfectly cooked and flavorful.
Action: Slice the chicken breasts and serve. *
