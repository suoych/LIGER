Step 1:
Image Description: Two medium-sized beetroots are being washed under running water, with a vegetable peeler removing the rough outer skin to reveal the deep red, smooth flesh of the beets.
Action: Wash and peel 2 medium-sized beetroots *

Step 2:
Image Description: The peeled beetroots are being finely grated on a grater. The vibrant red beetroot shreds pile up on a plate, looking soft and slightly moist.
Action: Grate the beetroots finely *

Step 3:
Image Description: A pan is on the stovetop with of oil heating up. The oil shimmers and spreads into a thin, glossy layer across the surface of the pan.
Action: Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan *

Step 4:
Image Description: Cumin seeds is added to the hot oil. The seeds begin to sizzle and splutter, releasing a warm, earthy aroma as they cook.
Action: Add 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds and let them splutter *

Step 5:
Image Description: Finely chopped onions are added to the pan, sizzling as they hit the hot oil. The onions slowly turn golden brown, softening and caramelizing as they are sautéed.
Action: Add 1 finely chopped onion and sauté until golden brown *

Step 6:
Image Description: A teaspoon each of minced garlic and ginger is added to the golden onions. The mixture is stirred together, releasing a fragrant aroma as the garlic and ginger sauté for another minute.
Action: Add 1 teaspoon of minced garlic and 1 teaspoon of minced ginger, and sauté for another minute *

Step 7:
Image Description: The finely grated beetroot is added to the pan with the sautéed onion, garlic, and ginger. The bright red beetroot is stirred in, blending with the mixture and beginning to cook down.
Action: Add the grated beetroot to the pan and stir well *

Step 8:
Image Description: A sprinkling of salt and turmeric powder is added to the beetroot mixture. The turmeric adds a subtle golden hue, and the beets continue to cook, softening and absorbing the flavors.
Action: Add salt to taste and 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder. Cook for 5 minutes *

Step 9:
Image Description: Cooked rice are added to the pan, mixing thoroughly with the spiced beetroot mixture. The rice turns a reddish color as it blends with the beetroot, becoming evenly coated.
Action: Add 2 cups of cooked rice to the pan and mix thoroughly *

Step 10:
Image Description: The beetroot rice is garnished with freshly chopped cilantro. The green cilantro leaves stand out against the deep red rice, adding a fresh touch to the vibrant dish.
Action: Garnish with some chopped cilantro *

Step 11:
Image Description: The beetroot rice is served hot on a plate, with steam gently rising from the dish. The rich red color of the beetroot rice is complemented by the fresh cilantro garnish, ready to be enjoyed.
Action: Serve hot *
