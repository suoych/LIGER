Step 1:

Image Description: Basmati rice is being rinsed in water until the water runs clear.
Action: Rinse 1 cup of basmati rice in water until the water runs clear. *

Step 2:

Image Description: The rinsed basmati rice is soaked in water, softening the grains before cooking.
Action: Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes. *

Step 3:

Image Description: Vegetables including an onion, tomatoes, carrot, and green beans are chopped, preparing them for cooking.
Action: Chop 1 onion, 2 tomatoes, 1 carrot, and a handful of green beans. *

Step 4:

Image Description: A pot is heated with 2 tablespoons of oil, and the chopped onion is fried until golden brown and caramelized.
Action: Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pot and fry the chopped onion until golden brown. *

Step 5:

Image Description: Ginger-garlic paste is added to the pot with the fried onions, sautéing until fragrant.
Action: Add 1 teaspoon of ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute. *

Step 6:

Image Description: Chopped tomatoes are added to the pot, cooking until they become soft and break down into the mixture.
Action: Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft. *

Step 7:

Image Description: Spices including biryani masala, turmeric, and salt are added to the softened tomato mixture, creating a flavorful base.
Action: Add 1 teaspoon of biryani masala, 1/2 teaspoon turmeric, and salt. *

Step 8:

Image Description: Chopped carrots and green beans are added to the pot, sautéed with the spiced tomato mixture.
Action: Add chopped carrots and green beans, sauté for 5 minutes. *

Step 9:

Image Description: The soaked basmati rice is gently stirred into the pot with the spiced vegetables, combining the ingredients.
Action: Add the soaked basmati rice and stir gently. *

Step 10:

Image Description: Water is poured into the pot, bringing the rice and vegetables to a boil.
Action: Pour in 2 cups of water and bring to a boil. *

Step 11:

Image Description: The pot is covered, and the rice is simmered on low heat until fully cooked and the water is absorbed, creating a fluffy vegetable biryani.
Action: Cover the pot and simmer on low heat until the rice is cooked and water is absorbed. *

Step 12:

Image Description: The vegetable biryani is garnished with freshly chopped cilantro and served hot.
Action: Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot. *
