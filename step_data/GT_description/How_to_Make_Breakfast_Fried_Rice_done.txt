Step 1:

Image Description: A large skillet is placed over medium-high heat with vegetable oil shimmering.
Action: In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. *

Step 2:

Image Description: Three beaten eggs are poured into the hot skillet. The eggs are scrambled gently until just set, then removed and set aside on a plate.
Action: Add 3 beaten eggs to the skillet and scramble until just set, then remove them from the skillet and set aside. *

Step 3:

Image Description: After the eggs are removed, an additional tablespoon of oil is added to the skillet. Diced onion and minced garlic are added and sautéed, releasing a fragrant aroma as they turn golden.
Action: Add another tablespoon of oil to the skillet and throw in 1 diced onion and 2 minced garlic cloves, sauté until fragrant. *

Step 4:

Image Description: One cup of diced ham is added to the skillet with the sautéed onions and garlic. The ham begins to brown slightly, adding texture and flavor.
Action: Add 1 cup of diced ham and cook until lightly browned. *

Step 5:

Image Description: Four cups of cold cooked rice are added to the skillet, and the clumps of rice are broken up. The rice mixes with the ham, onions, and garlic.
Action: Add 4 cups of cold cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps. *

Step 6:

Image Description: Soy sauce, oyster sauce, and a pinch of white pepper are poured into the skillet. The sauces coat the rice, turning it a light brown color as everything is stirred together.
Action: Pour in 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce, and a pinch of white pepper, then stir to combine. *

Step 7:

Image Description: The frozen peas and carrots, along with the previously scrambled eggs, are added back to the skillet. The mixture is stirred until everything is heated through, creating a vibrant fried rice.
Action: Add 1 cup of frozen peas and carrots mix and cooked eggs back to the skillet, stirring until everything is heated through. *

Step 8:

Image Description: The finished fried rice is garnished with freshly chopped scallions, adding a fresh pop of green and flavor just before serving.
Action: Garnish with chopped scallions before serving. *
