Step 1:
Image Description: Six eggs are placed in a saucepan filled with cold water. The water begins to boil, and the eggs cook as bubbles rise around them. The eggs sit covered in the hot water. The eggs are peeled and chopped into small pieces.
Action: Place 6 eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat. Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot water, cool, peel, and chop *

Step 2:
Image Description: The chopped eggs are placed in a mixing bowl with smooth mayonnaise and a teaspoon of bright yellow mustard. The ingredients are sitting together.
Action: In a bowl, combine the chopped eggs, 1/4 cup of mayonnaise, and 1 teaspoon of yellow mustard *

Step 3:
Image Description: Finely chopped green onions or celery (depending on preference) are added to the egg, mayonnaise, and mustard mixture. The fresh green color of the onions or celery contrasts with the pale, creamy egg mixture, adding crunch and flavor.
Action: Add 1/4 cup of chopped green onions or celery *

Step 4:
Image Description: The egg salad mixture is seasoned with salt and freshly ground black pepper, sprinkled evenly over the top. The salt and pepper add depth to the flavor.
Action: Season with salt and pepper to taste *

Step 5:
Image Description: The egg salad is mixed thoroughly, with all the ingredients well combined into a smooth, creamy consistency. The bowl is covered and placed in the refrigerator to chill, allowing the flavors to meld together.
Action: Mix well and refrigerate for at least 1 hour *

Step 6:
Image Description: The chilled egg salad is spooned onto slices of bread, spread onto crackers, or served in a leafy green salad. The egg salad has a creamy texture with bits of crunchy green onion or celery.
Action: Serve on bread, crackers, or in a salad as desired *
