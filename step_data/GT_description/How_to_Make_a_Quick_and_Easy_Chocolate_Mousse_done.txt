Step 1:

Image Description: A large mixing bowl is filled with 1 cup of heavy whipping cream. The cream is thick and smooth.
Action: In a large mixing bowl, add 1 cup of heavy whipping cream. *

Step 2:

Image Description: The whipping cream has been whisked into stiff peaks. The texture is light and fluffy, with the whipped cream holding its shape, creating peaks that stand tall in the bowl.
Action: Whip the cream until it forms stiff peaks. *

Step 3:

Image Description: In a separate bowl, chocolate chips are melted in the microwave. The chocolate is now smooth and glossy, stirred to remove any remaining chunks, creating a silky, liquid consistency.
Action: In a separate bowl, melt 1 cup of chocolate chips in the microwave for 30 seconds, stirring until smooth. *

Step 4:

Image Description: The melted chocolate is left to cool, reaching room temperature. The once glossy chocolate has now thickened slightly but remains smooth.
Action: Let the melted chocolate cool to room temperature. *

Step 5:

Image Description: The cooled, melted chocolate is gently folded into the whipped cream. The two ingredients slowly combine, creating a light and airy chocolate mousse with a smooth, rich texture.
Action: Gently fold the cooled chocolate into the whipped cream until well combined. *

Step 6:

Image Description: The chocolate mousse is being spooned into individual serving dishes. The mousse is light, fluffy, and smooth, filling each dish with a rich, velvety texture.
Action: Spoon the chocolate mousse into serving dishes. *

Step 7:

Image Description: The serving dishes filled with chocolate mousse are placed into the refrigerator to chill. The mousse will set and firm up during the chilling process.
Action: Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving. *

Step 8:

Image Description: The chilled chocolate mousse is garnished with a swirl of whipped cream and a sprinkle of chocolate shavings on top. The creamy texture contrasts with the rich chocolate, adding a final touch of elegance.
Action: Garnish with whipped cream or chocolate shavings, if desired. *
