Step 1:

Image Description: Fresh carrots are peeled and grated, creating fine carrot shreds.
Action: Peel and grate 3 cups of carrots. *

Step 2:

Image Description: The grated carrots are placed in a large mixing bowl, forming the base of the carrot salad.
Action: Place grated carrots in a large mixing bowl. *

Step 3:

Image Description: Plump raisins is added to the bowl with the grated carrots, creating a sweet and chewy contrast to the carrots.
Action: Add 1 cup of raisins to the bowl. *

Step 4:

Image Description: In a small bowl, mayonnaise is mixed with lemon juice, creating a tangy dressing for the salad.
Action: In a separate small bowl, mix 1/2 cup of mayonnaise and 2 tablespoons of lemon juice. *

Step 5:

Image Description: The creamy mayonnaise and lemon juice mixture is poured over the grated carrots and raisins in the larger bowl, ready to be combined.
Action: Pour the mayonnaise mixture over the grated carrots and raisins. *

Step 6:

Image Description: The ingredients are tossed together until the carrots and raisins are evenly coated in the creamy dressing, forming a well-combined salad.
Action: Toss the ingredients together until well combined. *

Step 7:

Image Description: The finished carrot and raisin salad is placed in the refrigerator to chill, allowing the flavors to meld before serving.
Action: Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving. *
