Step 1:

Image Description: A large pot is on the stovetop, heating coconut oil over medium heat. The oil is shimmering.
Action: In a large pot, heat 1 tablespoon of coconut oil over medium heat *

Step 2:

Image Description: Chopped onions are added to the pot, sizzling as they sauté in the coconut oil. The onions are softening and turning translucent as they cook.
Action: Add 1 chopped onion and sauté until translucent *

Step 3:

Image Description: Minced garlic and freshly grated ginger are added to the pot with the sautéed onions. The mixture is fragrant, and the garlic and ginger are sizzling.
Action: Add 3 cloves of minced garlic and 1 tablespoon of grated ginger, and sauté for another minute *

Step 4:

Image Description: A tablespoon of red curry paste is stirred into the pot. The curry paste is blending with the onions, garlic, and ginger, turning the mixture a rich red color.
Action: Add 1 tablespoon of red curry paste and stir well *

Step 5:

Image Description: Vegetable broth and a can of coconut milk are poured into the pot, creating a creamy, flavorful base for the soup. The broth and coconut milk blend into the curry mixture.
Action: Pour in 4 cups of vegetable broth and 1 can (14 oz) of coconut milk *

Step 6:

Image Description: Sliced mushrooms, chopped carrots, and chopped red bell pepper are added to the broth and coconut milk mixture. The vegetables are submerged.
Action: Add 1 cup of sliced mushrooms, 1 cup of chopped carrots, and 1 cup of chopped red bell pepper *

Step 7:

Image Description: The pot is brought to a boil, with the vegetables and broth bubbling gently. The heat is reduced, and the soup simmers, allowing the flavors to meld.
Action: Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes *

Step 8:

Image Description: Soy sauce, lime juice, and salt are added to the simmering soup. The seasonings balance the flavors, adding savory, tangy, and salty notes to the broth.
Action: Stir in 1 tablespoon of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of lime juice, and salt to taste *

Step 9:

Image Description: Chopped spinach is added to the soup, wilting quickly in the hot broth. The spinach blends with the other vegetables, adding a pop of green to the dish.
Action: Add 1 cup of chopped spinach and cook until wilted *

Step 10:

Image Description: The hot soup is ladled into bowls and garnished with freshly chopped cilantro and a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for extra heat. The dish is vibrant.
Action: Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh cilantro and red pepper flakes if desired *
