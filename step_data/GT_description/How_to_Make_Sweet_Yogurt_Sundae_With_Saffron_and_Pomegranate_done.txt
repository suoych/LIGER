Action: In a small bowl, soak a pinch of saffron threads in 1 tablespoon of warm water for about 5 minutes *

Step 2:
Image Description: A medium bowl filled with plain, creamy yogurt. The yogurt has a smooth, thick texture and is a pure white color, sitting evenly in the bowl.
Action: In a medium bowl, add 1 cup of plain yogurt *

Step 3:
Image Description: A spoonful of golden honey is being poured into the yogurt. The honey drips slowly, creating glossy streams across the surface of the yogurt. As it mixes in, the yogurt takes on a slight sheen from the honey’s sweetness.
Action: Stir in 1 tablespoon of honey into the yogurt *

Step 4:
Image Description: The small bowl of saffron and its vibrant yellow water is being poured into the yogurt mixture. The saffron threads and golden liquid mix with the yogurt and honey, creating swirling patterns as they blend together.
Action: Pour the soaked saffron (along with the water) into the yogurt mixture *

Step 5:
Image Description: The yogurt, honey, and saffron are being mixed thoroughly. The saffron colors the yogurt a soft, pale yellow, with streaks of golden orange from the saffron threads evenly distributed throughout. The yogurt now has a smooth, bright appearance.
Action: Mix well until the yogurt is evenly colored by the saffron *

Step 6:
Image Description: The saffron yogurt mixture is being spooned into serving bowls or glasses. The creamy, golden-yellow yogurt sits neatly in each container, with a smooth, even surface.
Action: Spoon the saffron yogurt into serving bowls or glasses *

Step 7:
Image Description: A generous sprinkle of bright red pomegranate seeds is added on top of the saffron yogurt. The seeds are glossy and juicy, contrasting beautifully against the pale yellow yogurt and adding texture and color to the dish.
Action: Sprinkle pomegranate seeds on top of the yogurt *

Step 8:
Image Description: A few fresh green mint leaves are gently placed on top of the yogurt and pomegranate seeds. The mint leaves add a pop of green and a refreshing touch to the dessert, complementing the bright red pomegranate seeds and creamy yellow yogurt.
Action: Garnish with a few mint leaves, if desired *

Step 9:
Image Description: The serving bowls or glasses of saffron yogurt, garnished with pomegranate seeds and mint leaves, are ready to be served. The vibrant colors—golden yellow, bright red, and green—create an inviting and elegant dessert.
Action: Serve immediately and enjoy your sweet yogurt sundae with saffron and pomegranate *
