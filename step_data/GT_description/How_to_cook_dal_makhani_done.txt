Step 1:

Image Description: Whole black lentils (urad dal) and kidney beans (rajma) are rinsed and soaking in a bowl of water overnight, softening for the next day’s cooking.
Action: Rinse 1 cup of whole black lentils (urad dal) and 1/4 cup of kidney beans (rajma), and soak them in water overnight. *

Step 2:

Image Description: The soaked lentils and kidney beans are drained and transferred into a pressure cooker.
Action: Drain the water and transfer the soaked lentils and beans to a pressure cooker. *

Step 3:

Image Description: Water, salt, and a pinch of turmeric are added to the pressure cooker, cooking the lentils and beans until they are soft.
Action: Add 4 cups of water, salt, and a pinch of turmeric, then pressure cook until they are soft. *

Step 4:

Image Description: In a separate pan, ghee or oil is heated, and chopped onions are sautéed.
Action: In a separate pan, heat ghee or oil and add chopped onions. *

Step 5:

Image Description: Ginger-garlic paste is added to the sautéed onions, cooking until the raw smell disappears, deepening the flavor.
Action: Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears. *

Step 6:

Image Description: Tomato puree, red chili powder, and coriander powder are added to the pan, creating a fragrant masala as it cooks until the oil separates.
Action: Add tomato puree, red chili powder, coriander powder, and sauté until the oil separates. *

Step 7:

Image Description: The soft cooked lentils and beans, along with their stock, are added to the rich tomato masala, combining into a flavorful dal base.
Action: Add the cooked lentils and beans along with their stock to the masala. *

Step 8:

Image Description: The dal mixture is stirred and left to simmer on low heat, allowing the flavors of the masala, lentils, and beans to meld together.
Action: Stir well and simmer on low heat, allowing the flavors to meld together. *

Step 9:

Image Description: Butter and cream are added to the dal, stirred in to give it a rich, creamy texture, perfecting the dal makhani.
Action: Add butter and cream, stirring until they are incorporated into the dal. *

Step 10:

Image Description: The dal makhani is garnished with freshly chopped cilantro and served hot.
Action: Garnish with freshly chopped cilantro and serve hot. *
