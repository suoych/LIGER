Step 1:
Image Description: Butter are melting in a pan, forming a golden pool. The butter starts to bubble gently as it heats up, creating a rich base for cooking.
Action: Heat 2 tablespoons of butter in a pan *

Step 2:
Image Description: A teaspoon of cumin seeds is sprinkled into the hot butter. The seeds splutter and release their earthy aroma as they cook, dispersing throughout the pan.
Action: Add 1 teaspoon of cumin seeds and let them splutter *

Step 3:
Image Description: Finely chopped onions are added to the pan with the butter and cumin seeds. The onions are being sautéed, turning from translucent to golden brown as they cook and caramelize.
Action: Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown *

Step 4:
Image Description: A spoonful of ginger-garlic paste is being added to the golden brown onions in the pan. The mixture sizzles as the paste is stirred in, releasing a strong, fragrant aroma.
Action: Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1-2 minutes *

Step 5:
Image Description: Pureed tomatoes are being poured into the pan with the onions, cumin seeds, and ginger-garlic paste. The bright red tomatoes blend into the mixture, and the sauce thickens as it cooks, with oil starting to separate from the edges.
Action: Add pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates *

Step 6:
Image Description: Turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala are being sprinkled into the tomato mixture. The spices are stirred in, deepening the color of the sauce and intensifying its flavor.
Action: Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala powder *

Step 7:
Image Description: A smooth, creamy cashew paste is being stirred into the spiced tomato-onion mixture. The sauce becomes thicker and richer, with a velvety texture forming as the cashew paste integrates.
Action: Stir in cashew paste and cook for a few minutes *

Step 8:
Image Description: Water is being poured into the pan to adjust the consistency of the curry. The mixture is stirred and brought to a boil, turning into a smooth and creamy curry base.
Action: Add water to adjust the consistency and bring it to a boil *

Step 9:
Image Description: Cube-cut pieces of paneer are added to the boiling curry. The paneer gently simmers in the sauce, absorbing the flavors.
Action: Add cube-cut paneer pieces and simmer for 5-7 minutes *

Step 10:
Image Description: A swirl of cream is added to the paneer curry, making the sauce even richer and creamier. The cream is mixed in, turning the curry into a smooth and luscious texture.
Action: Stir in cream and mix well *

Step 11:
Image Description: The Paneer Makhani is garnished with fresh coriander leaves, adding a pop of green color. The dish is hot and ready to serve, with the creamy sauce and paneer looking rich and inviting.
Action: Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot *
