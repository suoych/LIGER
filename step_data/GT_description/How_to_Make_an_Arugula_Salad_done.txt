Step 1:

Image Description: Fresh arugula are rinsed under cool water and gently dried, leaving the leaves clean and ready for the salad.
Action: Rinse and dry 4 cups of fresh arugula. *

Step 2:

Image Description: The freshly rinsed and dried arugula leaves are placed into a large salad bowl, forming the base of the salad.
Action: Place the arugula into a large salad bowl. *

Step 3:

Image Description: A half of a red onion is thinly sliced and added to the salad bowl, bringing a pop of color and crunch to the arugula.
Action: Thinly slice 1/2 red onion and add it to the bowl. *

Step 4:

Image Description: Cherry tomatoes are sliced in half and scattered into the salad bowl, adding a burst of sweetness and color.
Action: Slice 1 cup of cherry tomatoes in half and add them to the bowl. *

Step 5:

Image Description: A quarter cup of feta cheese is crumbled over the salad, adding a salty and creamy texture to the mix.
Action: Crumble 1/4 cup of feta cheese over the salad. *

Step 6:

Image Description: Toasted pine nuts are sprinkled over the salad, adding a nutty, crunchy element to balance the other ingredients.
Action: Add 1/4 cup of toasted pine nuts to the salad. *

Step 7:

Image Description: Olive oil and one tablespoon of balsamic vinegar are drizzled over the salad, providing a light dressing to enhance the flavors.
Action: Drizzle 2 tablespoons of olive oil and 1 tablespoon of balsamic vinegar over the salad. *

Step 8:

Image Description: The salad is lightly seasoned with salt and freshly ground pepper, adjusting the flavors to taste.
Action: Season with salt and pepper to taste. *

Step 9:

Image Description: The salad is gently tossed in the bowl, combining the arugula, onions, tomatoes, feta, pine nuts, olive oil, and balsamic vinegar to ensure every bite has balanced flavors.
Action: Toss the salad gently to combine all ingredients. *
