Action: Place the potato chunks into a large pot. *

Step 3:

Image Description: The pot is filled with water, covering the potato chunks completely.
Action: Fill the pot with water until the potatoes are fully covered. *

Step 4:

Image Description: A pinch of salt is added to the water in the pot with the potatoes.
Action: Add a pinch of salt to the water. *

Step 5:

Image Description: The pot of potatoes and water is brought to a boil over medium-high heat.
Action: Bring the pot to a boil over medium-high heat. *

Step 6:

Step 8:

Image Description: The drained potatoes are returned to the same pot after draining.

Step 9:

Image Description: Milk and butter are added to the drained potatoes in the pot.

Image Description: The mashed potatoes are seasoned with salt and pepper to taste.
