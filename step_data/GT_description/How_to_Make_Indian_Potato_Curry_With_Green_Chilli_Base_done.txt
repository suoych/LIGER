Image Description: Two large green chilies are finely chopped on a cutting board. The chilies are vibrant and add a touch of spice to the dish.
Action: Finely chop 2 large green chilies *

Step 3:
Image Description: Two tablespoons of oil are heating in a pan over medium heat. The oil is shimmering.
Action: Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan over medium heat *

Step 4:
Image Description: Mustard seeds are added to the hot oil, spluttering and releasing their aromatic essence into the pan.
Action: Add 1 teaspoon of mustard seeds and let them splutter *

Step 5:
Image Description: The chopped green chilies are added to the spluttering mustard seeds. The chilies sizzle in the hot oil, releasing their heat and flavor as they sauté for a couple of minutes.
Action: Add the chopped green chilies and sauté for 2 minutes *

Step 6:
Image Description: Chopped onions are added to the pan and sautéed until they turn golden brown, softening and caramelizing, blending with the spices.
Action: Add 1 chopped onion and cook until it turns golden brown *

Step 7:
Image Description: A teaspoon of turmeric powder and cumin powder are added to the golden onions, creating a vibrant yellow hue. The spices are stirred in, enhancing the aroma of the dish.
Action: Add 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder and 1 teaspoon of cumin powder, then stir well *

Step 8:
Image Description: The diced potatoes are added to the pan, and they are mixed thoroughly, ensuring they are evenly coated with the spices and flavors.
Action: Add the diced potatoes and mix well to coat them with the spices *

Step 9:
Image Description: One cup of water is poured into the pan, and the mixture comes to a boil. The water begins to blend with the spices, creating a rich broth for the potatoes.
Action: Pour in 1 cup of water and bring to a boil *

Step 10:
Image Description: The heat is reduced to low, and the pan is covered. The potato curry simmers gently, allowing the potatoes to absorb the flavors and become tender.
Action: Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and let it simmer for 20 minutes or until potatoes are tender *

Step 11:
Image Description: The potato curry is finished with salt to taste and garnished with fresh coriander leaves. The dish looks vibrant and flavorful, with the coriander adding a fresh green touch.
Action: Season with salt to taste and garnish with fresh coriander leaves *

Image Description: The finished potato curry is served hot, accompanied by rice or bread. The tender potatoes are coated in a rich, spiced sauce.
