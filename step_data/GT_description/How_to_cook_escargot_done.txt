Step 1:

Image Description: A modern metal oven is slightly open with the display showing 375°F. The interior oven light softly illuminates the empty metal racks inside, indicating the oven is warming up.
Action: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). *

Step 2:

Image Description: In a bowl, softened butter is mixed with minced garlic, finely chopped parsley, and a pinch of salt, forming a flavorful butter mixture.
Action: In a bowl, mix together 1/2 cup of softened butter, 2 cloves of minced garlic, 1/4 cup of finely chopped parsley, and a pinch of salt. *

Step 3:

Image Description: Escargots are arranged in a baking dish, ready to be topped with the garlic butter mixture.
Action: Place the escargots in a baking dish. *

Step 4:

Image Description: A spoonful of the garlic butter mixture is placed over each escargot, evenly coating them in the rich, flavorful butter.
Action: Spoon a small amount of the butter mixture evenly over each escargot. *

Step 5:

Image Description: The baking dish is covered with aluminum foil, ensuring the escargots cook evenly while retaining moisture.
Action: Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil. *

Step 6:

Image Description: The escargots are baking in the preheated oven, with the butter mixture bubbling and the escargots heating through.
Action: Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the butter is bubbling and the escargots are heated through. *

Step 7:

Image Description: The baking dish is removed from the oven, and the hot escargots are served with crusty bread, perfect for soaking up the garlic butter.
Action: Remove from oven and serve hot, preferably with a side of crusty bread. *
