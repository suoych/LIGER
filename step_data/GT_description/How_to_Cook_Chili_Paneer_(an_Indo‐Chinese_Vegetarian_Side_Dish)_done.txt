Step 1:

Image Description: Paneer is being cut into small, bite-sized cubes, ready for coating and frying.
Action: Cut 200 grams of paneer into bite-sized cubes. *

Step 2:

Image Description: A bowl holds cornflour and water mixed together, forming a smooth batter that will coat the paneer cubes.
Action: In a bowl, mix 2 tbsp of cornflour with water to form a batter. *

Step 3:

Image Description: The paneer cubes are dipped into the cornflour batter, fully coating each piece evenly for frying.
Action: Dip the paneer cubes into the batter and coat them well. *

Step 4:

Image Description: Oil is heating in a pan on medium heat.
Action: Heat 2 tbsp of oil in a pan on medium heat. *

Step 5:

Image Description: The coated paneer cubes are frying in the hot oil, turning golden brown and crispy on the outside. They are then removed and set aside.
Action: Fry the coated paneer cubes until golden brown and then remove them. *

Step 6:

Image Description: In the same pan, minced garlic is added and sautéed, releasing a fragrant aroma as it begins to brown.
Action: In the same pan, add 1 tbsp of minced garlic and sauté. *

Step 7:

Image Description: Chopped onions and sliced bell peppers are added to the pan with the garlic, being sautéed until softened and slightly caramelized.
Action: Add 1 chopped onion and 1 sliced bell pepper to the pan and sauté. *

Step 8:

Image Description: Soy sauce, ketchup, and vinegar are poured into the pan, mixing with the sautéed vegetables to form a savory sauce.
Action: Add 1 tbsp of soy sauce, 1 tbsp of ketchup, and 1 tsp of vinegar to the pan. *

Step 9:

Image Description: The sauce is stirred thoroughly, cooking for 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld and thicken slightly.
Action: Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes. *

Step 10:

Image Description: The fried paneer cubes are added back into the pan, ready to be tossed in the flavorful sauce.
Action: Add the fried paneer cubes back to the pan. *

Step 11:

Image Description: Everything is tossed together in the pan, ensuring the paneer is well coated with the sauce and mixed with the vegetables.
Action: Toss everything together until the paneer is well coated with the sauce. *

Step 12:

Image Description: The finished chili paneer is garnished with chopped spring onions and served hot.
Action: Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot. *
