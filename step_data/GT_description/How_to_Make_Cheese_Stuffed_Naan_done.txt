Step 1:

Image Description: A mixing bowl filled with all-purpose flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder, ready to be mixed.
Action: In a mixing bowl, combine 2 cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of sugar, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder. *

Step 2:

Image Description: Yogurt and vegetable oil are added to the dry ingredients, beginning to form the dough.
Action: Add 1/2 cup of yogurt and 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil to the dry ingredients. *

Step 3:

Image Description: Warm water is gradually added to the mixture, and the ingredients are kneaded together by hand or with a dough hook until a soft dough forms.
Action: Gradually add warm water and knead until you get a soft dough. *

Step 4:

Image Description: The soft dough is placed in a bowl, which is covered with a damp cloth, allowing the dough to rest and rise.
Action: Cover the bowl with a damp cloth and let it rest for 1-2 hours. *

Step 5:

Image Description: The rested dough is divided into small, even-sized balls, preparing them for rolling and filling.
Action: Divide the dough into small balls. *

Step 6:

Image Description: Each dough ball is rolled out on a floured surface into a small circle, creating the base for the cheese filling.
Action: Roll out each ball into a small circle. *

Step 7:

Image Description: A small amount of grated cheese is placed in the center of each rolled-out dough circle.
Action: Place a small amount of grated cheese in the center of the dough circle. *

Step 8:

Image Description: The edges of the dough are folded over the cheese, and the dough is sealed tightly to encase the cheese filling inside.
Action: Fold the edges over the cheese and seal the dough. *

Step 9:

Image Description: The filled dough balls are gently rolled out again to form flat, round cheese-stuffed naan, being careful not to burst the filling.
Action: Roll out the filled dough balls gently to form a flatbread. *

Step 10:

Image Description: The cheese-stuffed naan is cooked in a hot skillet over medium heat, turning golden brown on both sides as it cooks.
Action: Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the naan until golden brown on both sides. *

Step 11:

Image Description: The cooked naan is brushed with melted butter or ghee, giving it a glossy, rich finish.
Action: Brush the cooked naan with melted butter or ghee. *

Step 12:

Image Description: The hot cheese-stuffed naan is served fresh, the melted cheese inside creating a soft, gooey center.
Action: Serve the cheese-stuffed naan hot. *
