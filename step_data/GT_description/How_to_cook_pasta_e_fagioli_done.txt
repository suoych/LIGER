Image Description: All the ingredients for pasta e fagioli are laid out on a table: dry pasta, a can of beans, ripe tomatoes, whole garlic cloves, and onions. 
Action: Begin by gathering ingredients, including pasta, beans, tomatoes, garlic, and onions. *

Step 2:

Image Description: The garlic is finely chopped, and the onions are diced into small, even pieces. Both sit on a cutting board.
Action: Chop the onions and garlic. *

Step 3:

Image Description: A pot sits on the stovetop with olive oil beginning to shimmer as it heats over medium heat.
Action: In a pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat. *

Step 4:

Image Description: The chopped onions and garlic are added to the pot. They begin to soften and release their aroma as they sauté in the hot oil, turning slightly translucent.
Action: Add chopped onions and garlic to the pot, sauté until they are soft. *

Step 5:

Image Description: Canned tomatoes and their juices are poured into the pot over the sautéed onions and garlic, adding a rich red color and slightly chunky texture to the mixture.
Action: Add tomatoes and their juice into the pot. *

Step 6:

Image Description: The tomatoes start to break down, creating a smooth and slightly chunky sauce as they cook with the onions and garlic. The mixture begins to thicken.
Action: Stir and cook for a few minutes until the tomatoes break down. *

Step 7:

Image Description: Drained beans are added into the tomato mixture. The beans are firm but tender, mixing in well with the sauce.
Action: Add drained beans into the pot. *

Step 8:

Image Description: Broth or water is poured into the pot, filling it up as the mixture is brought to a boil. Small bubbles start to form on the surface.
Action: Pour in broth or water, and bring to a boil. *

Step 9:

Image Description: The pasta is added to the boiling mixture, which is then reduced to a simmer. The pasta begins to soften and absorb the flavors of the broth and tomatoes.
Action: Add pasta to the pot and reduce the heat to a simmer. *

Step 10:

Image Description: The ingredients are simmering together, and the pasta becomes tender as it’s stirred occasionally to prevent sticking. The broth thickens as the pasta cooks.
Action: Cook until the pasta is tender, stirring occasionally. *

Step 11:

Image Description: The finished pasta e fagioli is seasoned with salt and pepper. The flavors blend together, creating a warm, hearty dish.
Action: Season with salt and pepper to taste. *

Step 12:

Image Description: The hot pasta e fagioli is served in bowls, optionally garnished with freshly grated parmesan cheese and a sprinkle of fresh herbs like parsley or basil for a finishing touch.
