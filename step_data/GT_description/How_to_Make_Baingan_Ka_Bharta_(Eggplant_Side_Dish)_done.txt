Image Description: The eggplants are rubbed with a little oil, making the skin slightly shiny and preparing them for roasting.
Action: Rub the eggplants with a little bit of oil. *

Image Description: The oiled eggplants are roasted over an open flame or in the oven until the skin is charred and the flesh is soft, with the eggplants collapsing slightly.
Action: Roast the oiled eggplants over an open flame or in the oven until the skin is charred and the flesh is soft. *

Step 4:

Image Description: The roasted eggplants are allowed to cool before peeling off the charred skin, leaving the soft, smoky flesh to be mashed into a smooth consistency.
Action: Let the roasted eggplants cool, then peel off the charred skin and mash the flesh. *

Image Description: Two tablespoons of oil are heated in a pan, and cumin seeds are added, sizzling and spluttering to release their aromatic flavor.
Action: Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter. *

Step 6:

Image Description: Finely chopped onions are added to the pan and sautéed until they turn golden brown, becoming soft and caramelized.
Action: Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. *

Step 7:

Image Description: Finely chopped tomatoes are added to the pan, cooking down and becoming soft and mushy as they blend with the onions.
Action: Add finely chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy. *

Step 8:

Image Description: Minced garlic, grated ginger, and chopped green chilies are added to the onion-tomato mixture, sautéing for a couple of minutes to release their fragrant aromas.
Action: Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and green chilies, and sauté for a couple of minutes. *

Step 9:

Image Description: Turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt are added to the mixture, intensifying the color and flavor as the spices cook.
Action: Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt, and cook for another minute. *

Step 10:

Image Description: The mashed eggplant flesh is added to the spiced onion-tomato mixture, stirred well, and cooked, allowing the flavors to meld together.
Action: Add the mashed eggplants to the pan, mix well and cook for 5-7 minutes. *

Step 11:

Image Description: The dish is garnished with freshly chopped coriander leaves, adding a burst of color and freshness. 
Action: Garnish with chopped fresh coriander leaves and serve hot. *
