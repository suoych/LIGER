Image Description: A saucepan contains water, sugar, salt, and vegetable oil, all being combined and stirred together over medium heat. 
Action: In a saucepan, combine 1 cup of water, 2 1/2 tablespoons of sugar, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil *

Step 2:
Image Description: The mixture in the saucepan is brought to a boil. Small bubbles form as the liquid heats up, and the ingredients are fully dissolved.
Action: Bring the mixture to a boil over medium heat *

Step 3:
Image Description: After the saucepan is removed from the heat, flour is stirred into the boiling mixture. The dough thickens and forms into a ball as it’s mixed, pulling away from the sides of the pan.
Action: Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in 1 cup of flour until the mixture forms a ball *

Step 4:
Image Description: A deep fryer or large pot filled with oil is heated to 375°F (190°C). The oil shimmers, indicating it’s hot and ready for frying the churros.
Action: Heat oil for frying in a deep fryer or large pot to 375°F (190°C) *

Step 5:
Image Description: The dough is spooned into a piping bag fitted with a star-shaped tip. The bag is filled and ready to pipe the dough into hot oil.
Action: Spoon the dough into a piping bag fitted with a star tip *

Step 6:
Image Description: The dough is piped into the hot oil in long strips, creating the classic ridged churro shape. The dough begins to fry and puff up in the oil.
Action: Pipe strips of dough into the hot oil *

Step 7:
Image Description: The churros are fried until they turn a golden brown color. Once done, they are removed from the oil and drained on paper towels to absorb excess oil.
Action: Fry until golden brown and drain on paper towels *

Step 8:
Image Description: A shallow dish contains a mixture of sugar and cinnamon, evenly combined. The cinnamon adds a warm, sweet aroma to the sugar.
Action: Combine 1/2 cup of sugar and 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon in a shallow dish *

Step 9:
Image Description: The freshly fried churros are rolled in the cinnamon-sugar mixture, coating them evenly and giving them a sweet, crunchy outer layer.
Action: Roll the churros in the cinnamon-sugar mixture *

Step 10:
Image Description: The golden brown churros are served warm, with a crisp coating of cinnamon-sugar. A side of thick, rich chocolate sauce is placed alongside for dipping.
Action: Serve warm, optionally with a side of chocolate sauce for dipping *
