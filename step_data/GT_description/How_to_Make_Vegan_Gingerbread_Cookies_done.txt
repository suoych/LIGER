Step 1:
Image Description: A large mixing bowl with flour, baking soda, baking powder, ground ginger, ground cinnamon, ground cloves, and a pinch of salt. The dry ingredients are being whisked together, creating a fragrant, spiced mixture.
Action: In a large mixing bowl, combine 2 cups of flour, 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda, 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder, 1 teaspoon of ground ginger, 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon, 1/4 teaspoon of ground cloves, and a pinch of salt. *

Step 2:
Image Description: A separate bowl contains melted coconut oil, brown sugar, and molasses. The ingredients are being mixed together until smooth, forming a dark, rich, sweet liquid.
Action: In a separate bowl, mix 1/2 cup of melted coconut oil, 3/4 cup of brown sugar, and 1/4 cup of molasses until smooth. *

Step 3:
Image Description: A teaspoon of vanilla extract is being added to the wet mixture of coconut oil, sugar, and molasses. The vanilla is stirred in, enhancing the sweetness and depth of flavor.
Action: Add 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract to the wet mixture. *

Step 4:
Image Description: The dry ingredients are being gradually added to the wet mixture, while stirring continuously. The mixture thickens as the ingredients combine, forming a smooth gingerbread dough.
Action: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring continuously until a thick dough forms. *

Step 5:
Image Description: The thick gingerbread dough is being wrapped in plastic wrap. It’s now ready to be chilled in the refrigerator for at least an hour to firm up for rolling and cutting.
Action: Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour. *

Step 6:
Image Description: The oven is preheating to 350°F (175°C), with the display showing the temperature rising. The oven is ready for baking the chilled gingerbread cookies.
Action: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). *

Step 7:
Image Description: The chilled dough is rolled out on a lightly floured surface to about 1/4 inch thickness. The dough is smooth and ready to be cut into shapes.
Action: Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface to about 1/4 inch thickness. *

Step 8:
Image Description: Various cookie cutters are being used to cut festive shapes from the rolled-out dough. The shaped cookie dough is placed on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
Action: Use cookie cutters to cut out desired shapes and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. *

Step 9:
Image Description: The gingerbread cookies are baking in the oven, their edges turning lightly golden. The aroma of ginger, cinnamon, and cloves fills the kitchen as the cookies firm up.
Action: Bake the cookies for 8-10 minutes or until the edges are lightly browned. *

Step 10:
Image Description: The freshly baked gingerbread cookies are cooling on a wire rack. They are firm, golden-brown.
Action: Let the cookies cool completely on a wire rack before serving or decorating. *
