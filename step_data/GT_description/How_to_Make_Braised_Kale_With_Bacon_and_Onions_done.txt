Image Description: Six slices of bacon are chopped into small, even pieces, ready to be cooked. The bacon is raw, with visible marbling of fat and lean meat.
Action: Chop 6 slices of bacon into small pieces and set aside. *

Step 2:

Image Description: One large onion is sliced into thin, even strips, ready to be sautéed. The onion layers separate as they are sliced thinly.
Action: Slice 1 large onion into thin strips. *

Step 3:

Image Description: A large, empty skillet is placed on the stovetop, heating over medium heat, preparing for the bacon.
Action: Heat a large skillet over medium heat. *

Step 4:

Image Description: The chopped bacon is added to the heated skillet. The bacon begins to sizzle and release its fat as it cooks, turning crispy.
Action: Add the chopped bacon to the skillet and cook until crispy. *

Step 5:

Image Description: The crispy bacon is removed from the skillet using a slotted spoon, leaving the rendered bacon grease behind. The bacon is set aside on a plate, ready to be added back later.
Action: Remove the cooked bacon with a slotted spoon and set aside. *

Step 6:

Image Description: Thinly sliced onions are added to the hot bacon grease in the skillet. The onions sizzle and soften, absorbing the flavor from the bacon fat as they cook.
Action: Add the sliced onions to the skillet and cook in the bacon grease until softened. *

Step 7:

Image Description: Two bunches of kale are chopped into bite-sized pieces, with the tough stems removed. The bright green kale is ready to be added to the skillet.
Action: Chop 2 bunches of kale, removing the tough stems. *

Step 8:

Image Description: The chopped kale is added to the skillet, combining with the sautéed onions. The kale starts to cook down and soften in the hot pan.
Action: Add the chopped kale to the skillet. *

Step 9:

Image Description: The kale is stirred with the onions, wilting slightly as it cooks. The vibrant green leaves begin to soften and reduce in volume.
Action: Stir the kale until it starts to wilt. *

Step 10:

Image Description: One cup of chicken broth is poured into the skillet, mixing with the kale and onions. The liquid begins to steam and bubble slightly.
Action: Pour in 1 cup of chicken broth. *

Step 11:

Image Description: The skillet is covered with a lid, allowing the kale and onion mixture to simmer. The ingredients cook in the broth, softening and absorbing the flavors.
Action: Cover the skillet and let it simmer for 20 minutes. *

Step 12:

Image Description: The lid is removed, revealing the simmered kale and onions. Any remaining liquid evaporates as the mixture is stirred, leaving a slightly reduced, flavorful dish.
Action: Remove the lid, stir, and let any excess liquid evaporate. *

Step 13:

Image Description: The crispy bacon is added back into the skillet, mixed with the kale and onions. The bacon provides a savory, crispy texture to the softened vegetables.
Action: Add the cooked bacon back into the skillet and stir. *

Step 14:

Image Description: The finished dish is seasoned with salt and pepper to taste. The kale, onions, and crispy bacon are mixed together, ready to be served.
Action: Season with salt and pepper to taste, and serve. *
