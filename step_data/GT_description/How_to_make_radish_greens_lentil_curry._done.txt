Image Description: Minced garlic, grated ginger, and chopped tomatoes are added to the pan with the onions. The tomatoes soften as they cook, blending with the other ingredients.

Image Description: Freshly chopped radish greens are added to the pan. The greens wilt quickly, mixing with the spiced vegetable base.
Action: Add 2 cups of chopped radish greens and cook until wilted *
