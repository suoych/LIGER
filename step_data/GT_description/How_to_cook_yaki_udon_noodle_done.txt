Image Description: A pan with sliced onions, carrots, and bell peppers being stir-fried. The vegetables are beginning to soften, with the onions turning translucent, and the bell peppers and carrots slightly caramelized.
Action: Heat oil in a pan and add sliced onions, carrots, and bell peppers. Stir-fry until vegetables are softened. *

Step 3:
Image Description: The pan now includes sliced mushrooms and minced garlic, mixed in with the stir-fried vegetables.
Action: Add garlic and sliced mushrooms to the pan and continue to stir-fry. *

Step 4:
Image Description: The cooked udon noodles are added to the pan with the stir-fried vegetables and mushrooms. Everything is being tossed together, with the thick noodles intertwining with the vegetables.
Action: Add the cooked udon noodles to the pan and toss with the vegetables. *

Step 5:
Image Description: Soy sauce and mirin are being poured over the noodles and vegetables. The sauce is starting to coat the noodles, giving them a glossy, rich appearance.
Action: Pour in soy sauce and mirin, then stir everything together until the noodles are evenly coated. *

Image Description: The finished yaki udon is served hot, with a garnish of freshly sliced green onions and a sprinkle of sesame seeds on top, adding color and texture to the dish.
Action: Serve the yaki udon hot and garnish with green onions or sesame seeds if desired. *
