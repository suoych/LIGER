Step 2:

Image Description: The soaked chickpeas are drained and transferred into a food processor.
Action: Drain the chickpeas and transfer them to a food processor. *

Step 3:

Image Description: Onion, garlic, parsley, coriander, cumin, salt, and pepper are added to the food processor, combining with the chickpeas to create a flavorful base for the falafel.
Action: Add onion, garlic, parsley, coriander, cumin, salt, and pepper to the food processor. *

Step 4:

Image Description: The food processor pulses the mixture, turning the chickpeas and spices into a coarse paste, perfect for forming falafel.
Action: Pulse the mixture until it forms a coarse paste. *

Step 5:

Image Description: The falafel mixture is transferred to a bowl and placed in the refrigerator.
Action: Transfer the mixture to a bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes. *

Step 6:

Image Description: The chilled falafel mixture is being shaped into small balls or patties, ready for frying.
Action: Form the mixture into small balls or patties. *

Step 7:

Image Description: Oil is heating in a frying pan.
Action: Heat oil in a frying pan. *

Step 8:

Image Description: The falafel balls are frying in the hot oil, turning golden brown on all sides as they cook.
Action: Fry the falafel balls until golden brown on all sides. *

Step 9:

Image Description: The golden brown falafel balls are placed on paper towels to drain excess oil before serving.
