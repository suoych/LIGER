Step 1:
Image Description: Two tablespoons of olive oil are heating in a large pan over medium heat, shimmering and spreading evenly across the surface.
Action: Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large pan over medium heat *

Step 2:
Image Description: Finely chopped onions are added to the heated olive oil. The onions are cooking, becoming soft and translucent as they release their aroma.
Action: Add 1 finely chopped onion and cook until translucent *

Step 3:
Image Description: Two minced garlic cloves are added to the pan with the onions. The garlic cooks for another minute, blending with the softened onions and filling the kitchen with a fragrant aroma.
Action: Add 2 minced garlic cloves and cook for another minute *

Step 4:
Image Description: Arborio rice is added to the pan, and the grains are stirred to coat them with the oil and onion-garlic mixture. The rice begins to absorb the flavors, turning slightly glossy.
Action: Add 1 1/2 cups of Arborio rice and stir to coat the rice with the oil *

Step 5:
Image Description: White wine is poured into the pan, and the rice simmers as the wine is absorbed. The rice becomes more fragrant as it absorbs the wine.
Action: Pour in 1/2 cup of white wine and simmer until the wine is absorbed *

Step 6:
Image Description: Warm chicken or vegetable stock is added to the pan one ladle at a time. The Arborio rice is stirred constantly, absorbing each addition of liquid before more is added.
Action: Begin adding 4 cups of warm chicken or vegetable stock, one ladle at a time, stirring constantly and waiting until the liquid is absorbed before adding more *

Step 7:
Image Description: One cup of pumpkin puree is stirred into the risotto. The puree adds a rich, golden color and creamy texture, blending smoothly into the rice.
Action: Add 1 cup of pumpkin puree and stir to combine *

Step 8:
Image Description: The risotto continues to cook and thicken as the rice becomes creamy and tender. The mixture is stirred constantly to ensure even cooking and a smooth consistency.
Action: Continue to cook and stir until the risotto is creamy and the rice is cooked through *

Step 9:
Image Description: Grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper are stirred into the creamy risotto, enhancing the flavors and adding a rich, cheesy finish to the dish.
Action: Stir in 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper to taste *

Step 10:
Image Description: The finished pumpkin risotto is served hot, garnished with freshly chopped parsley for a pop of color and freshness. The dish looks creamy and inviting.
Action: Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired *
