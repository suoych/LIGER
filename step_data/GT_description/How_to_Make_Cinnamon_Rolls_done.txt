Step 1:
Image Description: A large bowl contains active dry yeast and warm milk, with small bubbles forming as the yeast activates in the liquid.
Action: In a large bowl, combine 2 and 1/4 teaspoons of active dry yeast and 1 cup of warm milk *

Step 2:
Image Description: Granulated sugar and melted butter are added to the bowl with the yeast and milk mixture. The ingredients are stirred together, creating a smooth, rich base for the dough.
Action: Add 1/2 cup of granulated sugar and 1/3 cup of melted butter *

Step 3:
Image Description: Two large eggs and salt are added to the bowl, blending into the yeast, sugar, and butter mixture, thickening it slightly.
Action: Add 2 large eggs and 1 teaspoon of salt *

Step 4:
Image Description: All-purpose flour is gradually added to the wet mixture, forming a soft dough as it’s mixed. The dough begins to come together into a slightly sticky, pliable texture.
Action: Gradually add 4 cups of all-purpose flour, mixing until a dough forms *

Step 5:
Image Description: The dough is being kneaded by hand on a lightly floured surface. The dough becomes smooth and elastic as it’s worked for about 5-10 minutes.
Action: Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5-10 minutes *

Step 6:
Image Description: The kneaded dough is placed in a greased bowl, covered with a cloth, and left to rise. The dough has doubled in size, becoming light and airy.
Action: Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it, and let it rise for about 1-1.5 hours or until it has doubled in size *

Step 7:
Image Description: The risen dough is rolled out into a large rectangle on a lightly floured surface. The dough is smooth and evenly spread.
Action: Roll out the dough into a large rectangle on a lightly floured surface *

Step 8:
Image Description: Softened butter is spread evenly across the surface of the rolled-out dough, creating a rich base for the cinnamon-sugar filling.
Action: Spread 1/3 cup of softened butter over the surface of the dough *

Step 9:
Image Description: A mixture of brown sugar and cinnamon is sprinkled over the buttered dough, coating it evenly with a sweet, spiced layer.
Action: Mix 1 cup of brown sugar and 2-3 tablespoons of ground cinnamon, and sprinkle the mixture evenly over the buttered dough *

Step 10:
Image Description: The dough is tightly rolled into a log shape, and it is sliced into 12-15 even pieces, creating spiral-shaped cinnamon rolls.
Action: Roll up the dough tightly into a log and cut it into 12-15 slices *

Step 11:
Image Description: The sliced rolls are placed in a greased baking pan. They are covered and left to rise again for about 30 minutes, becoming puffy and soft.
Action: Place the slices in a greased baking pan, cover them, and let them rise again for about 30 minutes *

Step 12:
Image Description: The cinnamon rolls are baking in the oven, turning golden brown on the top. The kitchen fills with the aroma of cinnamon and butter.
Action: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and bake the cinnamon rolls for 20-25 minutes or until golden brown *

Step 13:
Image Description: While the cinnamon rolls bake, a smooth, creamy frosting is made by mixing softened cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract. The frosting is thick and ready for spreading.
Action: While the cinnamon rolls are baking, prepare the frosting by mixing 4 oz of cream cheese, 1/4 cup of softened butter, 1-1.5 cups of powdered sugar, and 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract until smooth *

Step 14:
Image Description: The warm cinnamon rolls are topped with the creamy frosting, which melts slightly into the rolls, creating a luscious layer of sweetness.
Action: Spread the frosting over the warm cinnamon rolls *

Step 15:
Image Description: The frosted cinnamon rolls are served, looking soft and gooey with layers of cinnamon and sugar inside. The rich frosting adds a perfect finishing touch.
Action: Serve and enjoy! *
